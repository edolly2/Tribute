Babe Ruth

"The Sultan of Swat" "The Caliph of Clout" "The Behemoth of Bust" "The Great Bambino"

Timeline of Ruth's Life

1895

George Herman "Babe" Ruth, Jr. was born on Feb. 6th of this year to the parents Kate Schamberger-Ruth and George Herman Ruth, Sr. in a rough area of Baltimore, Maryland.

1902

When Babe was 7 years old his father sent him to St. Mary's Industrial School for Boys, a reformatory and orphanage, and signed custody over to the Catholic missionaries who ran the school.

1914

Babe signs his first Minor League baseball contract for $250 a month to the Baltimore Orioles. Later that year, Ruth makes his Major League debut for the Boston Redsox. Also, he marries Helen Woodford.

1918

Ruth appears in his first Major League World Series against the Chicago Cubs in which they ultimatly became victorious.

1919

Ruth was sold the New York Yankees.

1923

Ruth wins AL mvp and appears in his second World Series against the New York Giants, which again he came out on top.

1926

Babe appears in his third World Series against the St. Louis Cardinals where Babe and the Yankees swept the Cardinals in four games.

1928

Babe appears in his fourth World Series against the St. Louis Cardinals and once again were victorious.

1929

Babe Ruth Marries Claire Merritt Hodgson.

1935

Babe Ruth is sold to the Boston Braves and also plays in his last Major League game.

1946

Babe Ruth is diagnosed with a Malignant Tumor in his neck.

1948

Babe Ruth dies at age 53 from his battle with cancer.
